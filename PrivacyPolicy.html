Privacy Policy

Your Privacy and Trust Are Our Top Priorities

Our Privacy Commitment: Nobel Dispatcher LLC is committed to protecting the privacy and security of all registered clients and website visitors. We maintain strict confidentiality standards and never compromise your personal or business information.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Nobel Dispatcher LLC ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, register for our services, or communicate with us.

By using our website and services, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use our website or services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for an account on our website
  • Fill out forms (contact forms, registration forms, service requests)
  • Communicate with us via email, phone, or SMS
  • Submit documents or business information
  • Use our dispatch and logistics services

Types of Information Collected:

  • Personal Information: Full name, email address, phone number
  • Business Information: Company name, business address, DOT numbers, MC authority details
  • Contact Information: Mailing address, billing information
  • Communication Data: Messages, emails, and records of interactions with us
  • Service Information: Details about your trucking operations and service requirements

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing and managing our dispatch and logistics services
  • Communicating with you about your account, services, and updates
  • Processing payments and billing
  • Improving our services and customer experience
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Ensuring the security and integrity of our services
Important: We do NOT use your information for marketing purposes unrelated to our services, and we never share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Data Protection and Security

We take the security of your personal information extremely seriously. We implement and maintain industry-standard security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Security Measures Include:

  • Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure with firewall protection
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication protocols
  • Data backup and disaster recovery procedures
  • Employee training on data protection and privacy
Your Data is Safe: All registered clients' information is stored securely and treated as strictly confidential. We employ the same level of security measures that we use to protect our own business information.

5. Our Promise: No Unauthorized Data Sharing

We want to be absolutely clear:

  • We do NOT sell your personal information to anyone
  • We do NOT rent or lease your data to third parties
  • We do NOT share your information with third parties without your explicit consent
  • We do NOT use your data for purposes unrelated to providing our services

Your trust is essential to our business relationship, and we honor that trust by keeping your information completely confidential.

6. Third-Party Service Providers

We may use trusted third-party service providers to assist in operating our business and serving our clients. These providers have access to personal information only as necessary to perform specific tasks on our behalf.

Categories of Third-Party Tools:

  • Payment Processors: To securely process payments and billing
  • Communication Platforms: For email, SMS, and phone communications
  • Hosting Services: For website hosting and data storage
  • Analytics Tools: To understand website usage (anonymous data only)
  • CRM Systems: For customer relationship management

Important: All third-party providers are contractually obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information and are prohibited from using it for any other purpose.

7. SMS and Communication Consent

By providing your phone number and registering on our website, you consent to receive communications from Nobel Dispatcher LLC regarding your account, services, and important updates.

Communication Types:

  • Service-related notifications and updates
  • Load dispatch information and scheduling
  • Payment and billing reminders
  • Important account alerts
  • Customer service communications

Your Consent Rights:

  • Opt-In Required: We require explicit consent for marketing communications
  • Opt-Out Anytime: You can unsubscribe from non-essential communications at any time
  • Service Messages: Essential service-related communications may still be sent even if you opt out of marketing
  • SMS Rates: Standard message and data rates may apply based on your carrier

To opt out of marketing communications: Reply STOP to any SMS message, click the unsubscribe link in emails, or contact us directly.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and improve our services.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly (login, forms, security)
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytical Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website (Google Analytics)
  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser

Your Cookie Choices:

  • You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings
  • You may choose to disable cookies, though this may affect website functionality
  • Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are required for basic site operation

9. Your Rights and Choices

As a registered client and user of our services, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Right to Access:

You have the right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you. We will provide this information within 30 days of your request, free of charge for the first request within a 12-month period.

Right to Update/Correct:

You can update or correct your personal information at any time by:

  • Logging into your account and editing your profile
  • Contacting us directly with the updated information

Right to Delete:

You have the right to request deletion of your personal information. We will delete your data within 30 days of your request, except where we are required to retain it by law or for legitimate business purposes.

Right to Object/Restrict:

You can object to certain types of data processing or request that we restrict how we use your information. We will honor these requests unless we have compelling legitimate grounds to continue processing.

Right to Data Portability:

You have the right to request that we transfer your data to another service provider in a commonly used, machine-readable format.

How to Exercise Your Rights:

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section below. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

10. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Retention Periods:

  • Active Accounts: Information retained while your account is active and for 2 years after closure
  • Financial Records: Retained for 7 years as required by tax and accounting regulations
  • Legal Compliance: Information may be retained longer if required by applicable laws

After the retention period expires, your data is securely deleted or anonymized.

11. International Data Transfers

While we primarily serve US-based clients, some of our third-party service providers may process data in other countries. When we transfer your information internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

12. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will take immediate steps to delete that information.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, services, or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending an email notification to registered users
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this page

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

14. Legal Compliance

We comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations, including:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles (for international visitors)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) requirements
  • Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) for SMS communications
  • CAN-SPAM Act for email communications
  • Applicable state and federal privacy laws
